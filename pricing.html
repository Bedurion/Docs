Plans and editions

Plan answers “how much service?” Edition answers “for which type of guild?”

Free and Premium describe the service level. Community and Universal describe the product edition and the game data it understands.

  • Community Edition: built for Tibia guilds with characters, worlds, vocations and guild ranks.
  • Universal Edition: the game-independent product direction for other gaming communities.
  • Founder Edition: the private Lumina environment, not a public plan.
